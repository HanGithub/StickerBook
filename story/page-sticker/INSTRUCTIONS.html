How to use this book
Hi, I'm Roberto. Let's go on an adventure! When you see this picture use the stickers in the book to help me make new friends. Just put the stickers on the dashed outlines on the page. Then use the Roberto Storybook app to take a picture of the page with your smart phone or tablet computer. Roberto will act out the words on the stickers. Download our app at http://tidal.sesp.northwestern.edu/StickerBook
