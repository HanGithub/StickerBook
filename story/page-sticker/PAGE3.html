As he sat alone in the cold morning gloom, Roberto heard a squawk and yowl sound out in the alley. A truck horn add a low blast, and a church bell chimed in with the melody. Roberto swung his legs and drummed out a beat.

It wasn't his dad's band, but it was a tune. Roberto jumped to his feet, and before he knew it, he was dancing in the street. “Now, to find a friend,” Roberto decided.
