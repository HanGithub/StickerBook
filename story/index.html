Roberto

This is Roberto, and it's sad to say he just moved from his home ten miles away.

At his old home and on his old street he had many friends that he liked to go meet. They'd meet and they'd dance there on his old street, and he'd dance and he'd dance with his two dancing feet.

But as sat there alone in the cold morning gloom he suddenly started humming a tune. And he started to dance right there and right then, and he thought I'll go find new friends to dance with again.


Use the stickers to teach Roberto to dance.

So he marched to the corner and turned to his right and encountered a bird perched up on a light. “Hello there”, he smiled “do you like to dance? I'll be your new friend if you give me a chance.”

And then without waiting for a word of reply, Roberto started to dance as the old bird stood by. Roberto forgot all his worries as he danced and he hopped. He forgot his concerns and he forgot to stop!

Can you make Roberto dance without stopping?

You do too much dancing”, the bird suddenly squawked. “How could I dance with someone who won't ever stop?”

And watching the bird as it flew away, Roberto thought to himself, I won't be dismayed.

So he walked to the corner and turned to his right, and there were three cats, orange, black, and white. Three times Roberto gave a tip of his hat and performed his whole dance, once for each cat.

Can you make Roberto do his dance three times?

The cats all yawned and stuck their tails in the air. “Dance all you want, why should we care?”
